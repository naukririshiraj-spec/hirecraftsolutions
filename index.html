Our story

Built to serve businesses like yours

HireCraft Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a clear purpose: to give small and mid-size companies access to the same quality of HR expertise that large corporations enjoy — at a fraction of the cost.

We understand the unique challenges that growing businesses face — limited HR bandwidth, complex compliance requirements, and the pressure to hire fast. That's why we've built a suite of services that covers every aspect of human resources, from talent acquisition to statutory compliance.

Based in Udaipur, Rajasthan, our team brings deep domain expertise across industries and a hands-on approach that ensures your HR function runs smoothly — every single day.

"HireCraft was born from a simple belief — that every business, no matter how small, deserves world-class HR support. Our mission is to remove the complexity of compliance and hiring, so our clients can focus entirely on building their business and their people."

Rishiraj Singh Ranawat
Founder, HireCraft Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
